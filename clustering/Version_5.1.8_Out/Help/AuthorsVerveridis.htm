Curriculum Vitae

Dimitrios Ververidis supervised by Prof. Constantine Kotropoulos, obtained the Ph.D. while he was with the Artificial Intelligence and Information Analysis Lab (AIIA) in Informatics school of Thessaloniki. Dimitrios Ververidis has contributed into various fields of speech processing and pattern recognition such as speech emotion recognition from kids while they are immersed on a virtual earthquake environment, in speech segmentation to speaker classes, Gaussian mixture modelling of probability density functions, and speed and accuracy optimization for the Sequential Floating Forward Selection (SFFS) algorithm when selecting a subset of features that improves the classification rate of any pattern recognition problem. He has been employed in 3 research projects: a Greek project named as PENED 01ED312 Training Kids for earthquake situations with Virtual Reality, VISNET European network of excellence, and MUSCLE (Multimedia Understanding through Semantics, Computation, and Learning) European network of excellence.  His fields of interest are pattern recognition, speech processing, and multivariate statistical analysis.
